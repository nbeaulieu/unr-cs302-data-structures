About

Hello.

I'm a software engineer with over 33 years of industry experience, now serving as part-time faculty at the University of Nevada, Reno. My teaching focuses on making abstract concepts — data structures, algorithms, computational thinking — concrete and intuitive through visualization, active learning, and materials that meet students where they are.

I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering and bring real-world perspective from decades in software development. When I'm not in the classroom, you'll find me building interactive tools, mentoring students, and exploring how technology can make education more accessible.
